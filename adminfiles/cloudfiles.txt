cloud storage services is an area of active research. For example, such
schemes should provide user-friendly features that allow group members to
be conveniently added or removed, privileges of group members to be assigned
or revoked by authorized parties (e.g. group leaders), organizing of
members into one or more sub-groups, forming of (multiple) hierarchical
layers, etc. Specifically, privileges should be self-defined by group leaders,
